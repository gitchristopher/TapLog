Travel Information

Off-peak fares apply on:

  • weekdays: 8.30am–3.30pm and 7pm–6am the next day
  • weekends: all day
  • state-wide Queensland gazetted public holidays: all day.

Off-peak fares are based on when you touch on.

If you transfer between services on a multi-trip journey across peak and off-peak times, your go card will automatically calculate the fare as a combination of peak and off-peak.
